Privacy Policy for Reviewabot

At Reviewabot, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our GitHub App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of the Reviewabot GitHub App. The only thing we collect is general Pull Request information like the Git Diff. This diff is sent to Azure OpenAI.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your information in any way.

Disclosure of Your Information

As we do not collect any personal information, we do not disclose your information to any third parties.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
